Turn on developer mode:

1. Press the Home button on your TV’s remote.

2. Select Apps and click it.

3. Now press the Number button on your remote and type ‘12345‘ using the on-screen number.

4. You’ll then see the Developer mode pop-up. Highlight the On button and select it. Also input to "Host PC IP" your IP: (you should to be in the same network with TV)

5. Now select the OK option. The Developer mode will be enabled for the Apps section on your Samsung TV. Press and hold power button until you see logo SmartTV.

6. Wait 3-5 seconds after re-start of TV.

7. Next click to "Search TV" button below.

8. Please confirm on TV permissions to connect to your TV for the app.

9. Choose app which you would like to remove from a list

10. Click to remove.

Congratulations!
